Learn SDLC the fun way!

TeamNoClue's SDLC Study platform is a one stop place to learn all about the System Development Life Cycle, in a very fun and engaging way. This platform has been designed in a special way to enable the learners to progress in a very systematic way. Different components of the website together build an intuitive platform to grasp the most of the knowledge for long term. These components are as follows:

  • Study Area

    The Study Area contains all the resources to learn about SDLC and it's stages. It has been further divided in four parts: Introduction, Explanation, Example and Test it!. This makes the process of learning more fun and intuitive. Know More

  • Games

    You would have guessed it right, this section contains games that enriches the learning process and also help in embedding the newly learnt concepts into the memory for long term. Some of the games are: crosswords, puzzles, Scatters, Quizs, Kahoots! and Space game, cool ahh?

  • Devs

    This section is specially for those interested in the process behind the development of this platform. It describes the processes and the tools used. It also serves as a good example of SDLC.

  • Contact Us & About Us

    Feedback is our only way to improve and serve in a even better way. Please leave your feedback through the Contact Us page or connect with on social media from the About Us page.

Good Luck on your journey to learn SDLC with us.
